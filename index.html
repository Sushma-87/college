About us

Parappa Channapa Jabin PU College was established by KLE Society in the year 1957 to pursue quality education in Science. The college is named after principal donor Late Shri Parappa Channappa Jabin a philanthropist and a business man of Hubballi. The college gives top priority to academic excellence & aims at imparting quality education.

Emblem :

The ‘Sacred Bull’ is symbolic of service, the ‘flying bird’ is of awakened youth to take up any challenge. The ‘Hexagram of Veerashaiva Dharma’ is secular outlook in service of the people and the ‘Ishtalinga in a palm’ and the ‘halo’ around it is suggestive of knowledge to remove ignorance of the people and fulfillment of the Veerashaiva philosophy. The idea of the emblem is the union of Knowledge, Service and Sacrifice which are the base of Veerashaiva Dharma. The KLE Society gives educational service to the people irrespective of their caste, creed and region.

Vision :

To be the institution of choice for Science Education and Research, Manifesting excellence ,Stimulating evolutionary progress and Engaging the Society
